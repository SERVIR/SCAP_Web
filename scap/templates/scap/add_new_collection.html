Above Ground Biomass

Above ground biomass (AGB) estimations are expected to be expressed in Metric Tons of Carbon per Ha. This estimations are assumed to be valid for the calibration year they were produced for, and therefore applied for emission and carbon stock calculations for forest cover data in all following years.

Please provide reference information such as a comprehensive description, digital object identifiers (DOIs), and metadata links if available.
